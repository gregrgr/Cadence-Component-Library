2. Confirm the `.psm` and `.dra` files are under `E:\candence-sql\library\Cadence\Footprints`.
3. Confirm every required `.pad` file is under `E:\candence-sql\library\Cadence\Padstacks` or the footprint folder.
4. Confirm every required `.step` file is under `E:\candence-sql\library\Cadence\3D`.
